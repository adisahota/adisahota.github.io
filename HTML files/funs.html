Formula 1

Formula One is the pinnacle of motor-sport. It's a competition between the best teams with the great engineers, and the fastest and bravest drivers from around the world. Lets talk about the protagonists of F1 - the drivers. The drivers are amazing atheletes who risk their life everytime they step into the car.They need to stay extremely fit as even a 1-2 pounds of their body weight can set them back a tenth of of a second which is crucial factor to win in F1. The drivers can pull up to 3-4 G's of force everytime they go at 250km/h around a corner, and lose nearly 5-10 lbs every race.

In my opinion the greatest drivers of all time are:

  1. Lewis Hamilton
  2. Micheal Schumacher
  3. Fernando Alonso
  4. Aryton Senna
  5. Sebastian Vettel

My favourite F1 racecar, the 2020 Mercedes-AMG F1 W11 EQ Performance, it took thirteen wins, fifteen pole positions and won mercedes and Lewis Hamilton their 7th consecutive World Championship. It is also the one of the very few cars which could take the "Pouhon" corner of the Spa-francorchamps circuit flat out at 300km/h. For reference most F1 cars can only do 240-250km/h, 260km/h if the driver is brave enough to suffer 6 G's of lateral force on their neck.
